About Singh Kulwinder

ਰੱਬ ਵਰਗੀ ਮੇਰੀ ਮਾਂ ਨਹੀ,
ਮੇਰੀ ਮਾਂ ਵਰਗਾ ਰੱਬ ਹੋਵੇਗਾ!!



Bali Karyam Wala wrote a special song for Mother's Day about a mother's sacrifices. The song was sung by Singh Kulwinder and released by Amar Audio. The song was officially released on Youtube on April 10, 2015.

As of today, the song has over 55,000 views and over 400 "Likes". The song was shot in Karyam, Punjab with a small budget.
