Amarjit Singh(Bali Karyama wala) is a famous Punjabi lyricst,poet and whole time activist.
His famous Punjabi song Maa Warga Rabb,Neele Note,James Bond, Sajjana etc.

Amarjit Singh born on 15th April 1977 in Karyam District SBS Nagar (Nawanshahr) and His native village is also Karyam DistAmarjit Singh aka Bali Karyama Wala is a famous lyrics and a poet from Punjab, India.

Some of his hit songs include: Maa Warga Rabb, Neele Neele Note, James Bond and Sitare.  Bali Karyam has received many national awards for his songs.
